Get to Know me Better

Hello! I'm Souradeep Dey, the Owner & Founder of "Make your Wedding Canvas." Based in the vibrant city of Kolkata, I poured my passion into creating a company dedicated to transforming your most cherished moments into timeless, artistic memories. I believe every wedding deserves to be captured with a unique eye and heartfelt dedication, ensuring your canvas is as beautiful and personal as your love story.

Services

We specialize in capturing life's most precious moments with artistic vision and professional expertise. From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, we transform your special occasions into timeless memories. Our comprehensive services include wedding photography, pre-wedding shoots, traditional ceremonies, birthday celebrations, and custom album design. Every frame tells a story, and we're here to make yours unforgettable.
